# Nexus Security — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Nexus Security delivers industrial-grade managed cybersecurity services to mid-market enterprises across East Africa and globally. Operating on a monthly retainer model, we replace fragmented project-based security engagements with a single, predictable partnership covering defensive operations (24/7 SOC monitoring, incident response), proactive assurance (penetration testing, risk assessment, compliance management), and strategic infrastructure (cloud security, zero trust architecture, AI security). Headquartered in Kampala, Uganda, we bridge the gap between deep local context and world-class security standards.

## Problem

Mid-market enterprises in East Africa face a growing threat landscape but lack access to enterprise-grade security:

- **No local alternatives** — Global firms are too expensive and don't understand the East African regulatory and threat environment.
- **Fragmented engagements** — Security is bought project-by-project (a pentest here, a risk assessment there) with no continuous operations.
- **Compliance pressure** — Local data protection acts (Uganda DPPA, Kenya DPA) now mandate security controls that most firms can't implement alone.
- **AI risk blind spot** — As firms adopt AI/LLM tools, they lack the expertise to assess new attack surfaces (prompt injection, model poisoning, data leakage).

## Solution

A single monthly retainer that includes:

| Service Line | Offerings |
|---|---|
| **Defensive Operations** | 24/7 SOC monitoring, SIEM integration, log analysis, real-time threat detection, incident response & forensics |
| **Proactive Assurance** | Biannual penetration testing (external, internal, web app), AI penetration testing, risk assessment, compliance management (ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, local DPPA/DPA) |
| **Strategic Infrastructure** | Cloud security (AWS, Azure, GCP), zero trust architecture, AI application security |

## Target Market

- **Primary:** Mid-market enterprises (50–500 employees) in East Africa — Uganda, Kenya, Rwanda, Tanzania
- **Secondary:** Global mid-market firms requiring remote security operations
- **Verticals:** Fintech, banking, telecom, government contractors, healthcare, SaaS

## Business Model

- **Monthly retainer** — Predictable recurring revenue (12-month contracts)
- **Tiered pricing** — Based on environment size, number of assets, compliance requirements
- **Setup included** — No hidden fees; onboarding, agent deployment, and baseline assessment are part of the retainer
- **Extras at preferred rates** — Major cloud migrations, additional forensics, custom compliance audits

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Local presence, global standards** — We operate in East Africa and understand the threat landscape, regulatory environment, and business realities.
2. **Partnership model, not vendor** — One retainer, one point of contact, no handoffs. We know your environment because we monitor it daily.
3. **AI security specialization** — First-mover in AI/LLM penetration testing and AI application security for the East African market.
4. **Aligned incentives** — Risk reduction, not hourly billing. Our success is measured by your security outcomes.

## Marketing & Sales Strategy

- **Direct outreach** to CTOs, CISOs, and IT directors at target firms
- **Content marketing** — Threat briefings, compliance guides, AI security whitepapers
- **Partnerships** with cloud providers (AWS, Azure), ISPs, and legal firms specializing in data protection
- **Free initial assessment** — Security baseline review as lead generation

- **Locations:** Kampala (HQ), remote delivery worldwide
